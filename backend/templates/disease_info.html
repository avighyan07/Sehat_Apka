{% if selected == 'diabetes' %}

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body turns food into energy.

🔍 Causes
  • Genetics
  • Obesity
  • Physical inactivity
🧬 Symptoms
Increased thirst Frequent urination Extreme hunger Fatigue Blurred vision
⚠️ Complications
  • Kidney damage
  • Heart disease
  • Vision loss
🛡️ Precautions
  • Regular blood sugar checks
  • Healthy diet
  • Exercise
  • Medication compliance
🏃 Lifestyle
  • Low-carb meals
  • Walking or light workouts
🧪 Recommended Tests
  • Fasting blood sugar
  • HbA1c
  • Oral glucose tolerance
💊 Medicines
  • Metformin
  • Insulin
  • Sulfonylureas
👨‍⚕️ When to See a Doctor
  • Vision problems
  • High glucose readings
  • Fatigue with no cause
{% elif selected == 'heart' %}

Heart disease involves narrowed or blocked blood vessels that can lead to heart attacks.

🔍 Causes
  • High blood pressure
  • Smoking
  • Cholesterol buildup
❤️ Symptoms
Chest pain Shortness of breath Fatigue Irregular heartbeat
⚠️ Complications
  • Heart failure
  • Stroke
  • Cardiac arrest
🛡️ Precautions
  • Regular exercise
  • Low-sodium diet
  • Cholesterol checks
  • Blood pressure control
🏃 Lifestyle
  • Walking 30 mins daily
  • Stress management
🧪 Recommended Tests
  • ECG
  • Stress test
  • Angiography
💊 Medicines
  • Aspirin
  • Beta-blockers
  • ACE inhibitors
👨‍⚕️ When to See a Doctor
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath
  • Irregular pulse
{% elif selected == 'kidney' %}

Kidney disease involves damage that causes kidneys to filter blood improperly over time.

🔍 Causes
  • Diabetes
  • High blood pressure
  • Infections
🧬 Symptoms
Swelling in feet Fatigue Trouble sleeping Frequent urination
⚠️ Complications
  • Fluid buildup
  • Heart disease
  • Kidney failure
🛡️ Precautions
  • Limit salt intake
  • Stay hydrated
  • Monitor blood pressure
🏃 Lifestyle
  • Low-sodium foods
  • Hydration
🧪 Recommended Tests
  • Urine test
  • Creatinine test
  • GFR test
💊 Medicines
  • Diuretics
  • ACE inhibitors
  • Calcium channel blockers
👨‍⚕️ When to See a Doctor
  • Swelling in legs
  • Fatigue
  • Dark urine
{% elif selected == 'pneumonia' %}

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames air sacs in the lungs, causing cough and difficulty breathing.

🔍 Causes
  • Bacteria
  • Viruses
  • Fungi
🧬 Symptoms
Cough with phlegm Fever Shortness of breath Chest pain
⚠️ Complications
  • Sepsis
  • Respiratory failure
  • Pleural effusion
🛡️ Precautions
  • Vaccination
  • Hand hygiene
  • Avoid smoking
🏃 Lifestyle
  • Avoid cold exposure
  • Stay indoors if infected
🧪 Recommended Tests
  • Chest X-ray
  • Blood culture
  • Sputum test
💊 Medicines
  • Antibiotics
  • Fever reducers
  • Cough suppressants
👨‍⚕️ When to See a Doctor
  • High fever
  • Breathing difficulty
  • Persistent cough
